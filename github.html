About Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography, making it nearly impossible to counterfeit or double-spend. The most popular cryptocurrency is Bitcoin.

Bitcoin (BTC)

The first and most widely recognized cryptocurrency. A pioneer in blockchain technology.

Ethereum (ETH)

A decentralized platform that runs smart contracts, allowing developers to build decentralized applications (DApps).

Litecoin (LTC)

A peer-to-peer cryptocurrency created as a "lite" version of Bitcoin, offering faster transaction times.
